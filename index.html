Frequently Asked Questions

How does the project submission process work?

The project submission process is simple and straightforward:

Submit Your Project Details: Share your project requirements through our online form.
Get a Consultation: A mentor will provide you with a customized mentorship plan.
Collaborate with Our Mentors: Work closely with a mentor who will guide you through the project.
Track Progress: Monitor your project's progress through our student portal.
Complete and Submit: Finish and submit your project with confidence.
How long does it take to complete a project?

The time required to complete a project varies depending on the complexity and scope. Typically, projects are completed within 1-6 weeks.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the project?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not satisfied, we will review your concerns and provide additional support or revisions as needed.

How are payments handled?

Payments are handled securely through our online portal. We accept various payment methods including credit cards and online payment systems.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team within 7 days of receiving your project assistance for refund requests.
